    Smart Call Center


    Powered by Modzy AI and REV AI

    Help Hard of Hearing People to easily Extract Text from Audio Calls, Voice-notes etc. and run Audio/Voice Notes Texts Sentiments,Entities Analysis and Audio Text Contents Summarizations leveraging REV.AI and Modzy AI Models

    Application Features


    Upload Your Audio/Voice Notes


    Easily Upload Audio/Voice Notes, Extract Text from Audio/Voice Notes and run analysis of your Audio Calls, Voice Notes whether from Phones, Whatsapp, Facebook audios etc. leveraging rev.ai Speech to Text Recognition AI

    Audio/Voice Notes Text Sentimental Analysis


    Once Audio/Voice Notes has been Converted to Text Messages, The application automatically leverage Modzy Sentimental AI Model to detect and analyze audio calls text messages for Customers Moods, sentiments and message statements Positivity(Happy mood), Negativity(Angry mood) or Neutrality(Mild mood)

    Audio/Voice Notes Texts Entities Analysis


    The applications automatically leverages Modzy Entities Recognition AI Model. to detect the the major statements in the audio/voce notes mesages, names involves, persons , companies, organisations and entities involves in the audio Messages.

    Audio/Voice Notes Text Summarizations


    The applications automatically leverages Modzy Text Summary AI Model. to Summarize the Text contents of the Audio/Voice Notes Text Messages and makes it easily understandable for the Users by pointing to the user the main detail of the message and thereby saving Time and energy..
